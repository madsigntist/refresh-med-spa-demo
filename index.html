Lift & Tighten

Skin laxity can change how you feel in your own skin—but surgery isn’t your only option. Our noninvasive Lift & Tighten treatments use radiofrequency energy, laser technology, or microneedling (based on your skin needs) to stimulate collagen and gently firm the face, neck, or body. These techniques deliver noticeable definition with minimal downtime, preserving your natural expression while restoring structure and tone. At Refresh, we approach every treatment with precision, so your results look like you—just more lifted and refreshed.
